    5. HOW TO ENTER (NO PURCHASE NECESSARY):

  5. From July 21, 2016 to March 31, 2017 (the "Promotional Period") eligible WCR or PP members that have met the criteria set by OLG in its sole and absolute discretion ("Eligible Members") who visit the Booth at an Event during Event hours will have one (1) chance to participate in the Promotion per day. You can find a list of Events and Event hours at any participating Site.

  6. Eligible Members must provide consent for their photo to be used within the The Selfie Slot Machine Application (the "Application") in order to participate in this Promotion. Participants must complete and sign the Audio and Visual Consent Form that will be provided to the participant at the Booth during the Event.

    7. THE SELFIE SLOT MACHINEAPPLICATION:

  7. A brand ambassador will take the Eligible Member's photo and load it into the Application after consent has been obtained (as outlined in Rule 6). The photo will appear on a publicly displayed television screen at the Event. The photo will then be split into three (3) sections horizontally each of which capture the Eligible Member's eyes, nose and mouth respectively.

  8. The Application will begin to spin the three (3) sections and scramble the Eligible Member's photo among various OLG logos. Once the Application finishes spinning, the Eligible Member will be provided with a coupon for free play (each, a "Coupon" and collectively the "Coupons") in accordance with Rule 10. Coupons may be redeemed at any of the participating Sites during the Promotional Period. All Coupons expire on the dates and times as noted on the Coupon. Only one (1) Coupon per Eligible Member, per day, per Promotional Period may be redeemed.

If the Eligible Member's photo does not line up in this way, the Eligible Member will receive one Coupon for $5.00 (CAD).
